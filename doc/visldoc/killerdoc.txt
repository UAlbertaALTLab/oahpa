Dás leat njeallje cealkkačoakkáldaga main lea iešguđet sátneluohkkáválljen. 

Vearbasojaheapmi lea heivehuvvon dási mielde ná:
- introductory level: imperatiiva (dušše 2sg) ja indikatiiva (dušše preseansa).
- medium level: imperatiiva (dušše 2. pers), indikatiiva (sihke preseansa ja preterihtta) ja actio.
- high level: indikatiiva, imperatiiva ja kondišunála, buot persovnnain ja loguin. Maid passiiva ja muhtun infinihta hámit, nugo vearbaabeassiiva.

Dáin cealkkačoakkáldagain sáhtát maid válljet sojahit substantiivvaid ja pronomeniid. 

Dás lea maid sierra čoakkáldat mas don sáhtát sojahit vearbbaid potensiála modusis, dasa lassin sáhtát válljet adjektiivvaid, loguid, refleksiivapronomeniid ja substantiivvaid oamastangehčosiiguin. 

---
The game contains four sentence collections, each with different part of speech alternatives.

Verbal morphology is distributed in the following way:
- introductory level: imperative (only 2sg) and indicative (only present)
- medium level: imperative (only 2nd person), indicative (both present and past) and actio.
- high level: indicative, imperative and konditional, in all persons and numbers. Also passive and some infinite forms, such as verb abessive.

In these sentence collections you may also choose to inflect nouns and pronouns.

There is also a separate collection, where you may inflect verbs in the potential mood, adjectives, reflexive pronouns, and nouns possessive suffixes.

- Potentiála 3sg: Long forms, e.g. 'leažžá' and 'bođeža' instead of 'leš'/'leaš' and 'bođeš'.

Elsewhere, killerfiller uses western forms, e.g.
- Indikatiiva pr 1pl is 'boradit' instead of 'boradat'.
