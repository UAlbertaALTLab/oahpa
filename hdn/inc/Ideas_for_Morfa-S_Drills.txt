Morfa-S Drills
=======================================

UNIT 1
---------------

Verbs
------
Change between the basic form and the Simple Present form
LEMMA <--> +V+Simp+Pres


UNIT 2
-----------------

Verbs
-----
Change between the basic form and the Direct Past form
LEMMA <--> +V+Dir+Past 

Change between the Simple Present form and the Direct Past form
+V+Simp+Pres <--> +V+Dir+Past 


UNIT 3
-----------------

Verbs
-----
Change between the basic form and the Simple Future form
LEMMA <--> +V+Simp+Fut

Change between the Simple Present form and the Simple Future form
+V+Simp+Pres <--> +V+Simp+Fut

Change between the Direct Past form and the Simple Future form
+V+Dir+Past <--> +V+Simp+Fut


Nouns
------
Change between the Singular and Plural form of Kinterms
+N+Sg <--> +N+Pl
